Welcome to FIT Healthy, a professional fitness and wellness center in Sri Lanka, dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals through expert training, modern equipment, and a positive environment.

What We Offer

  • Aerobics — High-energy aerobic workouts to improve flexibility, coordination, and cardiovascular fitness.
  • Cardio Training — Effective cardio programs to burn fat, increase stamina, and improve heart health.
  • Gym Training — Fully equipped gym with modern machines and free weights for all fitness levels.
  • Fitness Consulting — Personalized fitness advice and training plans based on your body goals.
  • SPA & Recovery — Professional relaxation and recovery services to reduce stress and muscle fatigue.
  • Energy & Nutrition Food — Guidance on healthy Sri Lankan and international nutrition for energy and recovery.
